Ultimate Ronald - The Complete version!

Status: Totally Finished And Released! - (100%)

Privacy: Totally Public! - LEAKED!

Description:

Ultimate Donald, one of the most popular edits and one that I'm quite fond of, because thanks to this edit, mine exist!
So, wasn't there supposed to be an Ultimate Donald R/Ultimate Ronald?
Yes, the original author has created several versions, and in 2020 the last update was released
but... something is missing...
Do you know what it is?

Why did this edit come up?

Some years ago I had been given the idea, because the famous assistants, who accompany Ronald during the battle, are missing.
So, I decided to make my version to be able to add them and make them work 100%.
It has been a quite exhausting work, since I had to change several things in the code and make it compatible.
The result is quite gratifying, resulting in a final and complete version of Ultimate Ronald!
